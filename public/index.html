INFRASTRUCTURE
In·fra·struc·ture

noun

the basic physical and organizational structures and facilities (e.g., buildings, roads, and power supplies) needed for the operation of a society or enterprise.

Whether you realize it or not, infrastructure impacts your day to day life.

The growing demands of our society on our current infrastructure are increasing yearly. As we have started to see, the reduction of funding for maintaining our current infrastructure has begun to impact the quality of that infrastructure.

Governments of all levels will have some very tough choices when it comes to funding new and current infrastructure projects.
